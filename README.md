# 💫 About Me:
I enjoy building things at the intersection of AI, distributed systems, and real-world impact. Over the past few years, I've worked on everything from Swarm Learning and Federated AI to medical AI for side-effect prediction, RAG-based news summarization, and computer vision applications.<br><br>Lately, I've been spending a lot of time figuring out how machine learning models can collaborate without sharing data, how distributed systems stay in sync, and why they occasionally decide not to.<br><br>I'm especially interested in Machine Learning and LLM-powered applications. I'm always excited to collaborate on projects that combine research with practical implementation.<br><br>Currently, I'm exploring PyTorch, MLOps, and GenAI.
